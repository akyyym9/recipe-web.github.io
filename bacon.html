WHAT IS BACON WRAPPED SEA SCALLOP?

One of the simplest appetizer that I enjoy eating (and making) is Bacon Wrapped Sea Scallop. It is yummy and has a balanced flavor. The marriage between the bacon and scallop always works out great. In addition, it is easy to prepare. In fact, this recipe is intended for beginners.

I did not prepare any sauce for this dish. The garlic rub and the butter that I topped on the scallop is enough. However, feel free to add your favorite sauce to it. One good homemade sauce that I usually pair this with is ranch dressing. It is creamy with the right amount of spice.

If you want this dish to have a hint of lemon, please refer to a previous similar recipe that I shared. It is called Bacon Wrapped Scallops in Lemon Garlic Sauce.

WHAT ARE THE INGREDIENTS?
  • 9 to 12 pieces large sea scallops
  • 9 to 12 strips of bacon
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 14 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter sliced into small cubes
  • 2 teaspoons minced flat leaf parsley
INSTRUCTIONS
  • Slice the eggs in half lengthwise. Remove the egg yolk and put it in a bowl.
  • Mash the egg yolks using a fork.
  • Add the mayonnaise and Dijon mustard. Mix well.
  • Arrange the egg whites in a plate. Scoop equal amounts of the mixture into the center of each sliced eggs. Sprinkle some paprika on top.
  • Garnish with chopped dill.
  • Serve. Share and enjoy!
